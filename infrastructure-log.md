## Notes

- Replace the example rows above with your real dates and changes, then delete this note.
- Add a new row at the top of the table (just under the header) each time something physical changes — new hardware, cabling, cooling, power, rack layout, etc.
- If a change was driven by a problem (e.g. thermal issues, a node dropping offline), it's worth noting what the symptom was, not just the fix — that's the part that's useful in an interview.
